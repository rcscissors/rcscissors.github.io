Honoring Patsy Ticer

WHEN

Monday, March 19, 2018


WHERE

The George Washington Masonic Memorial


TIME

6:30 PM to 9 PM

Patsy Ticer was a legendary consensus-builder, vital to the health, growth, and success of Alexandria as a community and historic district.

Honor her place in history as a female trailblazer in politics this March 19 by joining the Friends of the Alexandria Commission for Women in celebrating her life and her achievements during a tribute at the George Washington Masonic Memorial.

Information about the event can be found on both the Alexandria Commission for Women's Facebook Page and the Friends of the Alexandria Commission for Women's Facebook Page.

You may purchase tickets online through PayPal or purchase tickets directly through the Friends of the Alexandria Commission for Women. For info on how to contact a member of the Friends, visit our contact page.
